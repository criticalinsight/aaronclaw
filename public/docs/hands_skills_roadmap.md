# Hands and Skills Evolution Roadmap (Phase 20+)

## Philosophy: Rich Hickey's Simplicity & Autonomy

Guided by Rich Hickey's principles, this roadmap focuses on de-complecting the 
"What" (Intent) from the "How" (Implementation). The goal is to move from 
**Assisted Autonomy** to **Transcendental Sovereignty**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Warning**: Avoid "complecting" the cross-factory signaling with specific 
> provider protocols. All communication must happen via the **AaronDB Fact Log**.

---

## Phase 20: Autonomous Integration & Shared Substrates [COMPLETE]
**Goal**: Transitioned from discrete Hands to a self-organizing Mesh
coordinated via AaronDB.

### [NEW] Hands
- `mesh-coordinator-hand`: Manages peer-to-peer Hand signaling and dependency resolution.
- `economic-balancer-hand`: Autonomous token budget reallocation based on ROI metrics from `Economos`.
- `substrate-integrity-warden`: Ensures no "complection" occurs in the shared AaronDB facts.

### [NEW] Skills
- `multi-provider-failover`: Ability to switch between Gemini, Anthropic, and local LLMs without intent drift.
- `autonomous-pr-curation`: Skill to prioritize and merge Architectura refactors without operator intervention.

---

## Phase 21: Cross-Factory Knowledge Synthesis
**Goal**: Secure, distilled knowledge sharing across independent AaronClaw nodes.

### [NEW] Hands
- `nexus-outreach-hand`: Discovers other Schematic factories and negotiates knowledge exchanges.
- `distillation-hand`: Compresses huge session logs into "Universal Truths" for the Knowledge Hub.

### [NEW] Skills
- `inter-factory-datalog`: Skill to query remote AaronDB instances via secure, zero-knowledge proofs.
- `domain-cross-pollinator`: Automatically maps patterns from one domain (e.g., Codebase Architecture) to another (e.g., Infrastructure Scaling).

---

## Phase 22+: The Transcendence Horizon
**Goal**: Meta-circularity and self-evolution.

### [NEW] Hands
- `demiurge-meta-hand`: A hand that can create new *Hands* and *Skills* by writing to `src/hands-catalog.ts` and triggering a rebuild.
- `aeturnus-consciousness-watch`: Monitors the infinite self-improvement loop for "Intent Drift" or "Recursive Hallucination."

### [NEW] Skills
- `compiler-design-skill`: Skill to optimize the system's own TypeScript runtime for Edge performance.
- `sovereign-ethics-engine`: A hard-coded skill to ensure self-evolution adheres to the core **Schematic Alignment Principles**.

---

## Rich Hickey Analysis
**Question**: Is the increased complexity of the Hand Mesh worth the utility?
**Answer**: Yes. By treating Hand communication as a "Shared Substrate" of immutable facts (AaronDB), we avoid the "complection" of direct RPC calls. The mesh becomes a simple consumer of facts, keeping the architecture decoupled.

> [!WARNING]
> **Hickey Warning**: The transition to Phase 22 (Meta-circularity) risks "Easy" vs "Simple". Designing a system that writes itself is "Easy" to start but "Complex" to maintain. We must ensure the *Demurge* hand writes **Simple** code.
